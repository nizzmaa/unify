### Project Description
Our project is a centralized campus communication platform that connects students and faculty within and across colleges to share notes, post academic updates, collaborate on projects, and engage in structured discussions. It enhances and organizes peer learning and event discovery prioritizing nearby colleges while reducing fragmented communication across multiple platforms.
### The Problem statement
Campus communication is scattered across multiple platforms, making it hard for students to find events, notes, and collaborators in one place.

### The Solution
Our platform solves this by providing a centralized, organized system for academic communication within and across colleges.
